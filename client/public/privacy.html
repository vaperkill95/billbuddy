Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

BillBuddy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (billbuddy.us).

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Financial Data: Bill amounts, due dates, payment history, and financial goals that you manually enter.
  • Bank Account Data: If you choose to connect your bank accounts through Plaid, we receive account balances, transaction history, and account metadata. We do not store your bank login credentials.
  • Device Information: We use Face ID/Touch ID for app security. Biometric data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our bill tracking and financial management services
  • To generate personalized spending insights and savings recommendations
  • To send bill reminders and payment notifications
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To improve our services and user experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Plaid: To securely connect to your bank accounts. Plaid's use of your data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication. Google's use of your data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): To power AI spending insights and the financial advisor feature. Your financial data may be processed by Anthropic's AI models to generate personalized advice.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available for additional account security
  • Face ID/Touch ID biometric protection on the mobile app
  • Bank credentials are handled exclusively by Plaid and never stored on our servers

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect linked bank accounts at any time
  • Disable two-factor authentication

Children's Privacy

BillBuddy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

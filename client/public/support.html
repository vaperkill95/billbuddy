Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my bank account?

Go to the Money tab and tap "Connect Bank." BillBuddy uses Plaid, a secure bank connection service used by thousands of financial apps, to safely link your accounts. We never store your bank login credentials.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. BillBuddy uses bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transfers. Your bank credentials are handled by Plaid and never touch our servers. The app also supports optional two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional security.

How do I add bills?

Tap the "+ Bill" button from any screen. Enter the bill name, amount, due date, and category. BillBuddy will track it for you and remind you when it's coming due.

How does income detection work?

BillBuddy analyzes your bank deposits over the last 90 days to identify recurring income like paychecks. Go to Money, then Income and tap "Detect from Bank" to automatically find your income sources.

Can I use BillBuddy without connecting a bank?

Absolutely! You can manually add bills, track income, set reminders, and manage your budget without connecting a bank account. Bank connection is optional and adds automatic balance tracking and transaction imports.

How do I delete my account and data?

Contact us at support@billbuddy.us with your account email and we will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

The app is not loading or showing errors.

Try closing and reopening the app, or check your internet connection. If the issue persists, please email us at support@billbuddy.us with a description of the problem and your device type.
